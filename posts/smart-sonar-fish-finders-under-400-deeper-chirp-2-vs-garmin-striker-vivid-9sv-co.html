Buying Guide

When selecting a smart sonar fish finder under 400, prioritize display quality, sonar technology, and mounting options that match your fishing style. Screen size and visibility are crucial – larger displays (7-9 inches) provide better detail and allow multiple sonar views, while vivid color technology ensures readability in bright sunlight. Consider CHIRP sonar with scanning capabilities for detailed underwater imaging, and GPS functionality for waypoint marking and mapping features.
